Meet the Team

Gbemisola Bamiduro (PhD)

Director of Finance & Adminstration

Dr. Gbemisola Bamiduro serves as the Director of Finance and Administration at the Drive Foundation for Leadership Advancement and Empowerment, where she leads the foundation’s financial strategy, organizational systems, and administrative operations. With a deep background in scientific research and a strong commitment to service, Gbemisola ensures that Drive’s mission is supported by solid infrastructure, responsible stewardship, and long-term sustainability.

In her role, Gbemisola oversees budgeting, grant management, compliance, and the day-to-day administration of the foundation’s growing portfolio of programs. From vocational training to digital empowerment initiatives, she ensures that every project at Drive is underpinned by sound financial planning and effective resource allocation.

Beyond her administrative leadership, Gbemisola is a dedicated scholar and researcher. She holds a Ph.D. in Environmental Chemistry, with her work focusing on the development of visible-light activated nanomaterials for the treatment of environmental pollutants. Her academic contributions have earned her recognition and awards from institutions such as the Indiana Academy of Science and the Air & Waste Management Association.

Gbemisola brings to Drive a rare combination of scientific rigor, organizational leadership, and a heart for impact. Her leadership helps to ensure that Drive Foundation is not only visionary in its goals—but also equipped with the systems and discipline to achieve them. She remains committed to building an organization that is accountable, efficient, and transformative for the communities it serves.
